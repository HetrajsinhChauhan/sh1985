The Gemstone Journey

From the depths of the earth to your collection. Explore our signature cuts and the meticulous process that brings a stone to life.

1

The Origin

Every masterpiece begins as a raw, uncut stone. We ethically source only the top 1% of gemstones, analyzing their inner structure to determine the perfect destiny.

2

Precision Cutting

Our master lapidaries study the stone's unique inclusions. Through mathematical precision and artistic intuition, the gemstone is cut to maximize light performance.

3

The Polish

The final phase where brilliance is born. Specialized diamond-dusted wheels create a mirror-like surface on every facet, revealing the gem's internal fire.

4

The Setting

Set into pure 18k gold or platinum by expert jewelers. The prongs are delicately folded to secure the stone while allowing maximum light exposure.
